A Short Note on GitHub 101

Post the pandemic time period, as companies battle through recession and other after effects of the pandemic, hiring the best talent will be the main motive of many companies, hence standing out will prove to be essential. GitHub 101 aims at fostering a better understanding of version control systems which are an essential aspect of the software industry. It is counted as a primary skill in addition to possessing knowledge on programming languages. Through the use of Git, students comprehend the various complexities regarding development in a team and understand how the effective use of Git, as a tool adopted by various companies for version control, can eliminate these complexities and facilitate a glitch free, non redundant workflow in the development lifecycle of a product.
